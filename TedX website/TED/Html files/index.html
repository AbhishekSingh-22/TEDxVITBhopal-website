TEDx VITBhopal
Imagine a day filled with brilliant speakers, thought-provoking video and mind-blowing conversation. An ultimate gathering that will unleash new ideas, inspire and inform. The TEDx stage has been a really impactful one that has enriched minds across nations, that has witnessed ideas that are worth sharing, and looking out for. A TEDx event is a local gathering where live TED-like talks and performances are shared with the community. TEDx events are fully planned and coordinated independently, on a community-by-community basis. The content and design of each TEDx event is unique and developed independently, but all of them have features in common.
